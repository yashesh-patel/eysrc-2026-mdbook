## Theme

A powerful hurricane has disrupted the city's emergency supply network.
A temporary **Military Supply Drop Zone** has been established to organise relief materials before they are distributed to affected regions.

Your autonomous **e-puck** robot must complete its calibration procedure by identifying and collecting emergency supply boxes placed around the deployment area.

## Why This Task Matters

Visual perception is one of the most important capabilities of an autonomous robot.
The concepts introduced in this task will prepare you for advanced topics such as line following, ArUco detection, wall following, and autonomous decision-making in the upcoming tasks.
