## Theme

The city has been partially stabilised after the initial wave of disasters.
However, a critical final phase of the rescue operation remains: a multi-environment evacuation spanning both underground tunnels and open urban zones.

Your **e-puck** robot must now bring together every skill it has learned: colour detection, line following, shape recognition, ArUco navigation, proximity sensing, and wall following - all in a single, unified mission.

---

## What You Will Demonstrate

This task is the culmination of the entire programme.
You will demonstrate mastery of all concepts introduced across Tasks 1 through 5:

- Camera-based colour and shape detection
- Line following with proportional control
- ArUco marker detection and decoding
- Finite state machine design
- Proximity sensor-based wall following
- Multi-phase autonomous behaviour

## Why This Task Matters

In real disaster-response robotics, a robot must handle dynamic, unpredictable environments across multiple operational phases.
The Final Task simulates this complexity and evaluates your ability to design a robust, fully autonomous system.
