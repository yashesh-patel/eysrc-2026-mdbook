<h2><center>What is Image Processing?</center></h2>
<hr>


Image processing is a way of using a computer to look at and understand pictures or videos. Just like our eyes and brain help us see and recognize things, image processing helps machines do the same. 
It takes an image, like a photo from a camera, and uses different techniques to make it clearer, find objects in it, or understand what’s happening. 
For example, it can help a robot see where a line is to follow it, or help detect colors and shapes. It’s a key part of teaching machines how to "see" and make decisions based on what they see.
